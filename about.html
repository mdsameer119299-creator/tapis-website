About TAPIS GLOBAL

Leading the world in premium flooring solutions with 20+ years of excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

🏆

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2000, TAPIS GLOBAL INTERNATIONAL PVT LTD has established itself as a premier provider of luxury flooring solutions. Our journey began with a simple vision: to deliver world-class flooring products that combine elegance, durability, and innovation.

Starting as a boutique carpet manufacturer, we have evolved into a globally recognized brand, serving prestigious clients across residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for discerning buyers worldwide.

Today, we operate from state-of-the-art facilities in Dubai and maintain partnerships with leading designers, architects, and interior specialists. Our collections represent the perfect fusion of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design.

🌟

Our Mission

To provide premium flooring solutions that enhance the beauty and functionality of residential and commercial spaces, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and customer service.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in luxury flooring, recognized for innovation, craftsmanship, and excellence. We aspire to create spaces that inspire and delight, setting the standard for quality and sophistication in the industry.
