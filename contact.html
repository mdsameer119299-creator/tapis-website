Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does installation typically take?

A: Installation timelines vary based on project scope. Residential projects typically take 1-3 weeks, while larger commercial projects may take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Q: Do you offer custom designs?

A: Yes, absolutely! We specialize in custom designs tailored to your specific requirements. Our design team works closely with clients to create unique, personalized solutions.

Q: What is your warranty policy?

A: We offer comprehensive warranties on all products. Carpets typically come with 5-year warranties, while tiles have 10-year warranties. Installation is guaranteed for 2 years.

Q: Do you provide maintenance services?

A: Yes, we offer professional maintenance and cleaning services. Our expert team can advise on care requirements and provide specialized cleaning for different materials.

Q: What is your delivery timeframe?

A: Standard products are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks. Custom orders may take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. International shipping is available to all countries.

Q: Do you offer consultation services?

A: Yes, we provide free consultation with our design experts. We can visit your space, assess your needs, and provide personalized recommendations without obligation.
