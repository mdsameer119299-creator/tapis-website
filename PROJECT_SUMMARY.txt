## 🔧 Customization Guide

### Quick Customizations

1. **Change Company Name**
   - Find: "TAPIS GLOBAL INTERNATIONAL PVT LTD"
   - Replace with your company name

2. **Update Contact Information**
   - Email: info@tapisglobal.com
   - Phone: +971-4-123-4567
   - Address: 123 Luxury Avenue, Dubai, UAE
   - Hours: Mon-Sat 9AM-6PM

3. **Change Gold Accent Color**
   - Edit CSS: `--accent-color: #d4af37;`
   - Or use: #FFD700, #FFC700, etc.

4. **Add Your Products**
   - Copy product card block in products.html
   - Paste and customize with your info

5. **Add Your Projects**
   - Copy project card block in projects.html
   - Paste and customize

6. **Add Images**
   - Replace emoji placeholders with images
   - See IMAGE_SETUP_GUIDE.txt for details
